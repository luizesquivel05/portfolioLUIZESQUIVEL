Luiz Esquivel - RPA Developer | Data Engineer

   My name is Luiz Esquivel, and I am a systems developer with a passion for creating innovative and efficient solutions. Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work in various companies and projects, honing my skills and gaining experience in different areas of systems development.

    I began my journey as a Web Developer at Impacta Jovem Brasil, where I deepened my knowledge in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Working in this dynamic and challenging environment allowed me to develop front-end skills and learn how to create attractive and responsive interfaces, as well as back-end skills using technologies like Node.js and PHP.

    Next, I joined Inove Telecom as an IT Intern, where I had the opportunity to work on team-based system development projects. During this period, I improved my development and data skills and gained valuable knowledge in databases, especially SQL and SQL Server, working with efficient data storage and retrieval. Continuing my journey at Inove Telecom, I was promoted to Systems Development Analyst. In this role, I was responsible for leading development projects from conception to implementation and maintenance. I worked closely with business and IT teams to understand customer needs and translate them into robust and scalable software solutions. During my time at Inove Telecom, I also developed advanced skills in modern Business Intelligence (BI) tools, including Power BI. These skills allowed me to extract valuable insights from data and provide relevant information for strategic decision-making.

    Additionally, I have had the opportunity to participate in significant projects. "Esquizando," a website I developed and maintain with care, demonstrates my passion for creating engaging web experiences. "Faculdade 05," another project where I put my back-end development knowledge into practice, allowed me to deepen my understanding of best practices in system architecture. One project I am particularly proud of is the "Sistema de Notas," a Python-based command-line interface (CLI) for grade calculations for teachers in different institutions. This project showcases my ability to create practical and efficient solutions using object-oriented programming and good software development practices. Furthermore, I developed the "Biblioteca LEsquivel05," a systems application with a graphical interface that utilizes a database to store the books I am reading. This project allowed me to enhance my GUI development skills and integration with databases, providing an intuitive experience for managing my personal library. Throughout these experiences, I have learned to work in teams, communicate ideas effectively, and tackle technical challenges creatively.

    My passion for programming, combined with my fast learning ability, allows me to keep up with the latest industry trends and technologies. I am looking forward to continuing my journey as a systems developer, taking on new challenges, and contributing to the creation of innovative, secure, and efficient solutions.
